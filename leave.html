Corus HR – Leave Management

Documentation for leave eligibility, application, and approval

Module: Leave Management | Audience: Admin, Manager, Employee | Last Updated: Dec 2025

Overview

The Leave Management module allows employees to request leave, managers to review and approve requests, and the system to automatically manage leave balances based on defined policies.
