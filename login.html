1. Login to Corus HR

Users can log in to Corus HR using the credentials shared after purchase or onboarding.

  • Enter your Username / Email
  • Enter your Password
  • Click the Login button
After successful login, you will be redirected to the Dashboard.

2. Forgot Password

If you are unable to remember your password, use the Forgot Password option.

  • Click Forgot Password on the login screen
  • Enter your registered Email ID
  • Click Submit
A password reset link will be sent to your registered email address.

3. Reset Password

After clicking the reset link from your email:

  • Enter a New Password
  • Confirm the New Password
  • Click Reset Password
Once reset is successful, you can log in using the new password.
