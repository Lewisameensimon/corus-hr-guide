Step-by-Step Explanation

1
Employee logs in to Corus HR.
2
Employee opens Performance module.
3
Employee sets goals and KPIs.
4
Manager reviews and approves goals.
5
Employee submits self appraisal.
6
Manager evaluates performance.
7
Final rating is approved.
8
Employee receives notification and records are stored.
